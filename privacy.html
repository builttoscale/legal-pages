Privacy Policy

BUILT TO SCALE

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026 | Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Built to Scale App LLC, a Pennsylvania limited liability company (“Built to Scale,” “the app,” “we,” “us”), provides a fitness training tracker for scaled athletes. This policy explains what data is collected, why, who processes it, where it is stored, how long it is kept, and what rights you have. It applies to the Built to Scale mobile app and the builttoscale.fit website. If you have questions or data requests, contact us at support@builttoscale.fit.

Users in Washington, Nevada, and Connecticut — and any other users of consumer health data: please also see our standalone Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy, which is linked from our website homepage and within the app and governs data covered by Washington’s My Health My Data Act, Nevada’s consumer health data law (SB 370), and Connecticut’s consumer health data provisions.

1. How the App Works (Privacy Context)

Built to Scale is local-first. Your workout records, movement history, and workout feel ratings live on your Device by default and are not transmitted anywhere unless you take a specific action. Cloud backup is a manual action you initiate — it does not happen automatically in the background.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Data (Supabase)

When you create an account, we collect your email address, used solely for authentication — to verify your identity and allow you to sign in. Your authentication token (the credential that keeps you signed in) is stored in your Device’s secure keychain via Capacitor Preferences and is not stored in plain text. It is used only to authenticate your requests to our servers and is not shared with any other party.

2.2 Fitness and Training Data (Supabase — Manual Backup Only)

When you choose to back up your data, the following is uploaded to our cloud servers: exercise records (movements, sets, reps, weights, distances, timing); workout structure and duration; workout feel ratings (your post-workout demand self-reports, from Very Easy to Very Hard); and app settings. This upload only occurs when you manually trigger it and, for consumer health data, only after you have given the separate affirmative consent described in our Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy. No fitness data leaves your Device without your explicit action. We do not sell, license, rent, or share your fitness data with any third party beyond the processors named in this policy. This information is used exclusively to provide the app’s functionality (trend signals, cross-device restore) and is never used for advertising, profiling, or sold to any third party.

2.3 Usage and Device Data (Firebase Analytics)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used at an aggregate level. Google automatically collects: device model and operating-system version; app version; device language and country; session duration and screen engagement; install source; and a Firebase installation ID — a pseudonymous, device-level identifier generated by Google that is not linked to your email address or your workout data and is not the same across all apps on your Device. We do not send names, email addresses, or fitness records to Firebase Analytics. Google’s data-processing terms apply; see https://policies.google.com/privacy. We do not track you: the app never requests or accesses your Device’s advertising identifier (IDFA), we use the Firebase Analytics configuration that collects no advertising identifiers, and analytics data is never linked with third-party data for advertising or shared with data brokers. You can disable analytics entirely using the in-app setting described in Section 8.

2.4 App Configuration (Firebase Remote Config)

The app contacts Google’s servers at startup and when returning to the foreground to receive configuration values and in-app messages (for example, notices about new features or maintenance). We do not include personal data in this request; it is a one-way pull of configuration values from Google’s infrastructure to your Device.

2.5 Feedback Submissions (Supabase; delivered via Resend)

If you submit feedback through the in-app feedback form, we collect: your email address (optional — only if you provide it, and used only to respond to your feedback); your feedback message text; and limited diagnostic context: operating system, device type, app version, the screen name where feedback was submitted, and a local pilot user ID. Feedback records are stored in Supabase and delivered to us by email through Resend, our email-delivery provider.

2.6 Website Contact Form (builttoscale.fit; delivered via Resend)

The builttoscale.fit website provides information about the app, a link to download it, and a Contact form. If you use the Contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you submit, used only to respond to your inquiry and delivered to us by email through Resend. The website does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies or similar tracking technologies.

3. Sources of Data

We collect data (a) directly from you — when you create an account, enter workouts, submit feedback, or use the website Contact form; and (b) automatically from your Device — through the Firebase Analytics SDK, as described above. We do not purchase personal information from data brokers or acquire it from other outside sources.

4. Why We Collect This Data

  • Email address — account creation and authentication, and to respond to feedback or Contact-form messages you send.
  • Fitness / training records — cross-device backup and restore, and in-app trend signals, when you choose to back up.
  • Device / usage telemetry — understanding app usage to improve performance and features.
  • Feedback and Contact-form submissions — responding to user-reported issues and improving the app.

5. Sensitive and Health-Adjacent Data

Your workout history — including exercise records, loads, timing, and workout feel ratings — is health-adjacent personal data, and certain state laws treat it as sensitive. We handle it with care: it is never sold to any third party; it is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing platforms; it is processed only by Supabase (for cloud storage) when you choose to back up; and it is not used to build profiles for purposes outside the app. Where a state law requires opt-in consent to collect or process this data — including Washington’s My Health My Data Act, Nevada’s SB 370, and Connecticut’s consumer health data provisions — we obtain that consent through a separate consent step before your first cloud backup, as described in our standalone Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy.

6. Who Processes Your Data

The following third-party processors may handle your data as described above, in each case under a data-processing agreement that limits their use of your data to providing services to us:

  • Supabase Inc. — cloud database (account data and manual backup) — https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Google LLC (Firebase) — analytics and remote configuration — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Resend (Resend, Inc.) — email delivery for feedback and website Contact-form messages — https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

We do not use advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing platforms.

7. How We Disclose Information; No Sale or Sharing

We disclose personal information only to the processors listed above, to operate the Service; when required by law or valid legal process; or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy. We do not “sell” your personal information, and we do not “share” it for cross-context behavioral advertising, as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act and similar laws. We do not engage in targeted advertising or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have some or all of the following rights: to know or access the personal information we hold about you; to delete it; to correct it; to obtain a portable copy; to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information and of targeted advertising and certain profiling; and to appeal a denial of a request. We do not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

You can exercise several of these rights directly in the app:

  • Delete your account. Open Built to Scale, tap Settings, scroll to the Account section, tap Delete Account, and confirm. Deleting your account permanently removes all data associated with your account, including your workout history and any cloud backup, from our servers. This action cannot be undone.
  • Export your data. The app includes a local data-export feature that lets you download your workout history to a file at any time, independent of cloud backup.
  • Opt out of analytics — any platform. The app includes an analytics setting (Settings → Privacy → Analytics) that disables Firebase Analytics collection on your Device, on both iOS and Android. Because we do not track you, iOS will not show an App Tracking Transparency prompt for this app — there is no tracking to allow or decline.

To make any other request, or to appeal a decision, contact us at support@builttoscale.fit. We will verify your request using the email associated with your account and respond within the time required by applicable law (generally 45 days, extendable where permitted). You may use an authorized agent to submit a request where the law allows.

9. Transparency at the Point of Collection

At or before the point of collection — for example, at sign-up and near the website Contact form — we provide a link to this policy so you can see the categories of information we collect and the purposes for which we use them before you provide it.

10. Where Your Data Is Stored

Account and backup data is stored on Supabase-managed cloud infrastructure located in the United States. Firebase Analytics and Remote Config data is processed on Google’s infrastructure in accordance with Google’s data-processing terms. All workout data is stored locally on your Device first and remains there unless you initiate a backup.

11. How Long We Keep Your Data

Account and fitness data (Supabase) is retained until you delete your account, at which point it is deleted from our cloud servers. Feedback and Contact-form messages are retained only as long as needed to address your inquiry and for reasonable recordkeeping. Analytics data (Firebase) is retained for fourteen (14) months under our configured Google Analytics retention setting [confirm configured value], per Google’s Analytics data-retention settings (https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/7667196).

12. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit, storage of your authentication token in your Device’s secure keychain, and access controls on our cloud database. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Data Breach Notification

If we become aware of a breach of security affecting your personal information, we will notify you and any applicable regulators as required by law, including Pennsylvania’s Breach of Personal Information Notification Act and the breach-notification law of your state of residence. In addition, to the extent the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule (16 C.F.R. Part 318) applies to us, we will provide the notifications it requires — notice to you and to the Federal Trade Commission — if there is a breach of security involving your identifiable health information, including any unauthorized acquisition or disclosure without your authorization.

14. Minors

The Service is intended for adults 18 and older and is not directed to children. Account creation requires you to affirmatively confirm that you are 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe someone under 18 has created an account, please contact us at support@builttoscale.fit and we will delete the account and any associated data.

15. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. For material changes to how we handle sensitive or health-adjacent data, we will provide prominent notice and, where required, seek your consent. Continued use of the app after non-material changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

16. Contact

For privacy questions, data-deletion requests, or data-access requests: support@builttoscale.fit.
