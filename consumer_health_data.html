Consumer Health Data Policy

BUILT TO SCALE

Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy

(Washington My Health My Data Act; Nevada SB 370; Connecticut Data Privacy Act health provisions)

Effective Date: July 19, 2026 | Last Updated: July 19, 2026

This is a standalone Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy. It is linked prominently from the builttoscale.fit homepage and from within the Built to Scale app. It applies specifically to “consumer health data” as defined by Washington’s My Health My Data Act, Nevada’s consumer health data law (SB 370, codified in NRS chapter 603A), and Connecticut’s consumer health data provisions (Conn. Gen. Stat. § 42-515 et seq.). It describes the consumer health data we collect, how we use and share it, and the rights you have. Built to Scale App LLC is the entity responsible for this data. This policy applies to you if you are a Washington resident or a natural person whose consumer health data is collected while you are physically in Washington, if you are a Nevada resident or your data is collected in Nevada, or if you are a Connecticut resident. We honor the same practices for all other users.

1. What Is Consumer Health Data

“Consumer health data” means personal information that is linked or reasonably linkable to you and that identifies your past, present, or future physical or mental health status. For Built to Scale, this includes information about your exercise and physical activity.

2. Consumer Health Data We Collect; Your Consent

We collect the following categories of consumer health data, and only when you choose to enter it and to back it up to our cloud servers:

  • Exercise and activity records — movements, sets, repetitions, weights, distances, and timing.
  • Workout structure and duration.
  • Workout feel ratings — your post-workout demand self-reports (from Very Easy to Very Hard).

By default this information stays on your Device. It is only transmitted to us if you manually initiate a cloud backup. Before your first cloud backup, we ask for your affirmative, opt-in consent to collect and store this data on our servers, through a consent screen that is separate from your acceptance of the Terms & Conditions and the Privacy Policy and that describes the categories of data collected, the purposes, and the recipient (Supabase, our storage processor). If you do not consent, your data simply remains on your Device and is not transmitted to us. We keep a record of your consent. We do not collect geolocation data, and we do not use geofencing of any kind.

3. Sources of Consumer Health Data

We collect consumer health data directly from you — the information you enter into the app. We do not derive it from other sources, purchase it, or receive it from data brokers or third parties.

4. How We Use Consumer Health Data

We use consumer health data solely to provide the app’s functionality: to store and display your workout history, to generate in-app trend signals, and to enable cross-device backup and restore when you choose to back up. We do not use it for advertising, for profiling outside the app, or for any purpose unrelated to providing the Service.

5. How We Share Consumer Health Data

We share consumer health data only with the processor that stores it on our behalf: Supabase Inc., our cloud database provider, located in the United States. Supabase processes this data under a binding data-processing agreement that restricts its use of your data to providing storage and backup services for us and prohibits any use for its own purposes. We may also disclose consumer health data if required by law or valid legal process, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy.

Categories of third parties and affiliates

  • Processors: Supabase Inc. (cloud storage and backup).
  • Affiliates: none. Built to Scale does not share consumer health data with any affiliate.
  • Third parties to whom we sell consumer health data: none.

6. We Do Not Sell Consumer Health Data

We do not sell your consumer health data, and we will not sell it, without first obtaining your valid authorization as required by law. We do not exchange consumer health data for money or other valuable consideration.

7. Your Rights Regarding Consumer Health Data

You have the right to:

  • Confirm and access — confirm whether we are collecting, sharing, or selling your consumer health data, and access that data. Our response to an access request will include a list of all third parties and affiliates with whom we have shared your consumer health data — currently only Supabase Inc. (support@supabase.io) — together with contact information for them.
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw your consent to our collection and sharing of your consumer health data. You can withdraw consent at any time by disabling cloud backup in Settings and deleting your cloud backup, or by contacting us; withdrawal stops all further collection by us.
  • Delete — have your consumer health data deleted.

You can exercise these rights directly in the app: use the local data-export feature to access your data, and use Settings → Account → Delete Account to permanently delete your account and all associated consumer health data, including any cloud backup, from our servers. You may also contact us at support@builttoscale.fit to make any of these requests or to withdraw consent. We will verify your request using the email associated with your account and respond within the time required by law. If we deny a request, you may appeal by replying to our decision; if your appeal is denied, you may contact the Attorney General of your state: the Washington State Attorney General’s Office (Washington), the Nevada Attorney General (Nevada), or the Connecticut Attorney General (Connecticut).

8. Security and Retention

We protect consumer health data with reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including encryption in transit and access controls on our cloud database. We retain consumer health data until you delete your account, after which it is deleted from our servers.

9. State-Specific Notes

Washington. This policy is the consumer health data privacy policy required by RCW 19.373. It covers Washington residents and any natural person whose consumer health data is collected in Washington.

Nevada. This policy serves as the policy required by Nevada SB 370; Nevada residents have the rights described above, enforced by the Nevada Attorney General.

Connecticut. We process Connecticut consumers’ health data only with opt-in consent, as required by the Connecticut Data Privacy Act; Connecticut residents may exercise the rights above and appeal as described.

10. Contact

To exercise your rights or ask questions about this policy, contact us at support@builttoscale.fit.
